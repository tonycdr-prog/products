# Product Brief — Visibility Lite

## What it does
Affordable SEO visibility tracker with a simple visibility score and AI‑mention checks for small sites.

## Who it’s for
Indie founders and small marketing teams priced out of Semrush/Ahrefs.

## Core features (MVP)
- Visibility score calculator
- Lightweight rank tracking (planned)
- AI mention snapshots (manual inputs initially)

## Pricing strategy
- Starter £9/mo
- Growth £19/mo
- Agency £49/mo

## Differentiation
- Price-sensitive audience focus
- Simple, opinionated metrics
