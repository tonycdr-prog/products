# TypeTrace — Product Brief

## What it does
Captures and recalls typed text locally (not just clipboard). Users can search and copy previous snippets. MVP is in‑browser; desktop apps later.

## Target users
- Writers, developers, support agents
- Anyone who loses text before copying

## Core value prop
Recover typed text safely without spyware.

## Pricing strategy
- Free: last 50 snippets
- Starter: £4/mo (unlimited history)
- Team: £15/mo (policy controls)

## MVP scope
- In‑browser capture from a typing area
- Auto-split on Enter or idle
- Search + copy
- Email waitlist
