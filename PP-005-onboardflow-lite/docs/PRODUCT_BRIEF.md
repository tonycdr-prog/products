# OnboardFlow Lite

## What it does
OnboardFlow Lite is a lightweight embeddable onboarding wizard for B2B SaaS teams. It captures structured onboarding inputs, saves drafts with resume tokens, and generates a customer-success handoff pack that can be copied or exported as JSON.

## Target customer
- B2B SaaS founders and small GTM teams
- Customer success and implementation teams onboarding trial or newly closed accounts
- Teams currently using generic forms (Typeform/Tally/Google Forms) but needing partial-save and better handoff quality

## Pricing model
- **Starter (£15/mo):** 1 active form, draft/resume links, local handoff export
- **Growth (£49/mo):** multi-form templates, hosted persistence, team handoff history, integrations
- Future expansion: enterprise plan with SSO, permissions, and SLA support

## Competitive advantage
1. **Resumable onboarding by default** (token-based resume flow)
2. **Built-in CS handoff output** (not just form responses)
3. **Fast embed model** for founder-led teams that need to ship onboarding flows quickly
4. **Opinionated qualification scoring** to help prioritize high-quality accounts immediately
