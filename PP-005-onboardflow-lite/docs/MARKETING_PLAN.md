# Marketing Plan

## Landing page headline
**“Stop losing onboarding leads halfway through forms — let customers save, resume, and hand off cleanly.”**

## 3 benefit bullets
- **Recover drop-offs:** Save drafts and resume onboarding without restarting
- **Better team handoffs:** Auto-generate a structured CS handoff summary from form data
- **Launch quickly:** Embed in any site with a simple iframe snippet

## CTA copy
**Primary CTA:** “Start onboarding with resume links”

**Secondary CTA:** “See a live handoff pack demo”

**Indie Hackers (founder pain-angle post)**
