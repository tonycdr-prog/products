# RepairTrail — Product Brief

## What it does
A tenant-controlled repair log and escalation pack generator.
- Log repair issues with severity, reported date, who was contacted, and notes
- Shows a timeline with “overdue” heuristics
- Generates an escalation letter embedding the timeline
- Exports a PDF pack (timeline + checklist + letter)

## Who it’s for
- UK tenants dealing with ignored repairs (security, leaks, damp, heating)
- People who need a clear evidence timeline before escalating

## MVP scope
- Local-only repair log (localStorage)
- Timeline UI + overdue badges
- Letter generator
- PDF export pack

## Pricing strategy
- Free: log + copy/paste
- £9 one-off: PDF export pack
- £6/mo: reminders + multi-property + cloud backup
