# RepairTrail — Marketing Plan (first week)

## Positioning
"Keep a repair log that you can actually use. Timeline + escalation pack in one click."

## Distribution channels
1. Reddit: r/HousingUK, r/LegalAdviceUK (value-first: share a template + checklist; avoid spam)
2. Shelter/CAB adjacent communities (Facebook groups)
3. SEO: "landlord not fixing", "repair log template", "how to complain repairs letting agent"
4. Partnerships: tenant advocacy orgs, renters unions
5. TikTok/YouTube shorts: “How to document disrepair properly”

## First-week tactics
- Publish a free downloadable “repair evidence checklist”
- Post anonymised example timeline and explain why it matters
- Ask 10 renters for field additions (photos, contractor visits, council contact)

## What to measure
- Issues logged per user
- Export-to-PDF rate
- Waitlist conversions
