# Product Brief — HMRC CGT Multi‑Disposal Reporting Helper

## What it does
Interactive calculator that handles **multiple disposal dates**, **annual exempt allocation**, and **basic‑rate band usage** for UK CGT. Produces per‑disposal totals and an exportable CSV summary.

## Target customer
UK investors, brokers, and small accountants who need a fast way to estimate CGT when disposals span multiple dates.

## Pricing model
£9–£15 one‑off per calculation or £5/mo for ongoing access.

## Competitive advantage
Purpose‑built for multi‑disposal reporting (not just a single‑sale calculator), works offline, and provides a clean per‑disposal breakdown + CSV export.

## Status
MVP calculator is live in `index.html` with editable rows, tax band handling, and CSV export.
