# Product Brief — HMRC CGT Multi‑Disposal Reporting Helper

## What it does
Interactive calculator that handles **multiple disposal dates**, **annual exempt allocation**, and **basic‑rate band usage** for UK CGT. Produces per‑disposal totals and an exportable CSV summary.

## Target customer (refined)
UK retail investors and small advisory firms who make several disposals per tax year and need a fast, audit‑ready CGT estimate before submitting to HMRC.

## Pricing model (refined)
- **Free:** Unlimited in‑browser calculations + CSV export
- **Pro:** £9/mo for saved scenarios, PDF export, and annual tax‑year reminders

## Competitive advantage
Purpose‑built for multi‑disposal reporting (not just a single‑sale calculator), works offline, and provides a clean per‑disposal breakdown + CSV export.

## Distribution tactic (realistic)
Publish a “UK CGT multi‑disposal checklist” post and share it in r/UKPersonalFinance, r/UKInvesting, and r/UKTax, linking to the free calculator as the first step.

## Status
MVP calculator is live in `index.html` with editable rows, tax band handling, and CSV export.
