# StackSpend Guard Marketing Plan

## Landing Page Headline
Stop AI tool sprawl before the invoice lands.

## Benefits
- Find forgotten trials, duplicate subscriptions, and unowned AI tools in one five-minute audit.
- Generate a copy-ready AI spend policy for Slack, Notion, or finance review.
- Export Markdown and CSV reports without connecting a bank account or installing software.

## CTA Copy
Run the free AI spend audit — no signup, no data leaves your browser.

## Primary Distribution Channel
LinkedIn operator/agency founder posts with teardown screenshots: show a messy AI stack, run the audit, then share the generated policy and savings target. Offer to run five free audits for teams in the comments/DMs.

## First-Week Validation Targets
- 50 free audit completions.
- 10 conversations with agencies or operators spending £300+/month on AI tools.
- 3 paid pre-orders for £29/month alerting or £99/month agency reporting.

1. “The AI subscription spreadsheet every 8-person team needs.”
2. “How much should a small team spend per head on AI tooling?”
3. “The forgotten trial checklist for AI tools.”
