# Marketing Plan — WhatsApp Booking Guard

## Channels
1. Reddit (r/smallbusinessowner, r/tours): ask for feedback with demo.
2. Facebook groups for tour operators.
3. Google Ads: “WhatsApp booking tool”.
4. Partnerships with local tourism associations.
5. YouTube Shorts: 30s before/after booking workflow.

## First-week tactics
- DM the original thread with a beta link.
- Offer free setup for first 5 operators.
- Publish case study: 4 hours/day saved.
- Post to WhatsApp Business communities.
- Collect 3 operators for testimonials.
