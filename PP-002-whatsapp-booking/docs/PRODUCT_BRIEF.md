# Product Brief — WhatsApp Booking Guard

## What it does
Simple slot manager for small tour operators to prevent overbooking and speed up WhatsApp replies. Generates ready-to-send templates and tracks capacity.

## Who it’s for
- Tour guides, small events, local experiences
- UK operators who rely on WhatsApp for bookings

## Pricing strategy
- Starter £15/mo (basic slot manager)
- Growth £45/mo (team access, reminders)
- Pro £89/mo (WhatsApp API integration)

## Why now
WhatsApp is the default for many small operators, but booking volume quickly becomes unmanageable. A lightweight tool avoids heavy marketplace software.
