# StackDetect Lite — First-week Marketing Plan

## Distribution channels (5)
1. Hacker News: Show HN (tool) + Ask HN answers when people ask “what stack is this?”
2. Reddit: r/webdev, r/SaaS, r/startups (tool drop + transparent MVP limitations)
3. SEO: “detect tech stack”, “wappalyzer alternative”, “detect openai on website”
4. Cold outbound: agencies/SDRs (offer bulk trial)
5. Integrations: Chrome extension later (one-click scan)

## First week tactics
- Day 1: launch Lite as free; capture emails.
- Day 2: write a blog post: “Why Wappalyzer misses AI vendors (and what to look for).”
- Day 3: post a comparison video on X/LinkedIn.
- Day 4–7: outreach 30 agencies with “paste-mode works even if the site blocks scrapers” angle.

## KPI targets
- 500 visits
- 30 waitlist signups
- 5 Pro demos
