# Product Brief — PermaQR

## What it does
Generates a QR code and a self‑hosted redirect page so printed QR codes can point to a permanent, editable destination.

## Who it’s for
- Authors and creators with printed materials
- Small businesses with packaging/flyers
- Event organisers and artists

## Pricing strategy
- DIY: free download
- Hosted (£3/mo): hosted redirect + basic analytics
- Studio (£9/mo): multiple links + QR packs

## Core value
Prevent expensive reprints by owning the destination URL.
