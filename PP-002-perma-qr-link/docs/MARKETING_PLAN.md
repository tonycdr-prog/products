# Marketing Plan — PermaQR

## 5 distribution channels
1. Reddit (r/webdev, r/selfpublish, r/marketing)
2. Creator YouTube channels (print-on-demand, self-publishing)
3. SEO: "permanent QR code", "link in bio QR" queries
4. Etsy seller forums/groups
5. Pinterest/Instagram creator communities

## First-week launch tactics
- Post "printed QR survival kit" guide
- Offer free redirect template + domain checklist
- DM 10 creators with printed QR codes for feedback
- Publish a comparison table vs dynamic QR services
- Run a Product Hunt “Maker Tools” launch
