# CreditGuard AI — Product Brief

## What it does
Tracks AI API usage errors, estimates refundable credits, and generates dispute-ready summaries for provider support teams.

## Who it’s for
- Ops/FinOps teams using AI APIs
- Startups with significant AI spend

## Core features
- Refund estimator
- Error tracking + ticket log
- Multi-provider summaries

## Pricing strategy
- Ops £29/mo
- FinOps £99/mo

## Differentiation
- Focused on dispute handling (not just usage dashboards)
