# Product Brief — Maintainly

## What it does
Maintainly is a lightweight maintenance scheduler for small landlords. It tracks recurring tasks (boiler service, filter changes, PAT tests), nags on overdue items, and stores completion logs in one place.

## Target users
- UK small landlords with 1–50 units
- Property investors who don’t want a full property management suite

## Core value
- Prevents missed maintenance that causes costly failures
- Keeps compliance evidence tidy (receipts, notes, dates)

## Pricing strategy
- Starter £12/mo (up to 10 units)
- Pro £29/mo (up to 50 units)
- Agency £79/mo (unlimited units + API)

## MVP scope
- Task list + due dates
- Overdue highlighting (“nag mode”)
- Simple completion log
- Email waitlist capture
