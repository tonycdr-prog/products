# Product Brief — Maintainly

## What it does
Maintainly is a lightweight maintenance scheduler for small UK landlords. It tracks recurring safety tasks (boiler service, PAT tests, smoke alarms), nags on overdue items, and keeps a simple completion log for compliance.

## Target customer
- UK landlords managing 1–50 units
- Self‑managing property investors who don’t want a full property management suite
- Small letting agencies who need a simple compliance log

## Core value
- Never miss statutory maintenance deadlines
- Overdue tasks stay visible until completed
- Clean audit trail for inspections or insurance

## Pricing model
- **Free:** 1 property, up to 10 active tasks
- **Pro:** £9/mo — unlimited properties + compliance export

## Distribution tactic
Publish a “Landlord maintenance calendar (UK)” checklist and share it in NRLA community posts + LandlordZONE forums, linking to Maintainly’s free tier for immediate use.
