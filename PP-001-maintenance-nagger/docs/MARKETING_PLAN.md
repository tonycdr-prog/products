# Marketing Plan — Maintainly

## Positioning
"The lightweight maintenance nagger for landlords who hate bloated property management software."

## 5 distribution channels
1. r/uklandlords + r/RealEstate value post: maintenance checklist template + Maintainly waitlist
2. UK landlord Facebook groups (compliance/maintenance reminders)
3. Property investor newsletters (guest post: "Avoid costly maintenance misses")
4. Partnerships with local HVAC/boiler service firms (affiliate referrals)
5. Google Search ads: "landlord maintenance schedule" / "property maintenance tracker"

## First-week launch tactics
- Day 1: Post free maintenance calendar template + waitlist CTA
- Day 3: Share short demo video (30–45s) on LinkedIn + landlord groups
- Day 5: Offer free migration of existing spreadsheets for first 10 signups
- Day 7: Publish "UK landlord compliance checklist" blog post
