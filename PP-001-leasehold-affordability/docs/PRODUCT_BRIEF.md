# LeaseholdCalc — Product Brief

## What it does
A UK-focused affordability calculator for leasehold flats that **includes service charge, ground rent, insurance, and a buffer** when estimating:
- a safe “all-in housing” monthly budget
- the mortgage payment budget after leasehold running costs
- approximate max mortgage + max purchase price

## Who it’s for
- UK first-time buyers / upsizers looking at leasehold flats (London heavy)
- Buyers comparing multiple flats with wildly different service charges
- Mortgage brokers who want a quick screening link for clients

## Why now
Service charges have become unpredictable (inflation, remediation, insurance spikes). Buyers are increasingly anxious about recurring costs that lenders and listing portals often under-emphasize.

## MVP scope
Static site that runs entirely in the browser. Saves scenarios + waitlist emails in localStorage.

## Pricing strategy
- Free: 1 scenario, basic outputs
- Plus £4/mo: unlimited scenarios, projections, export
- Broker £49/mo: client sharing + white-label PDF exports

## Monetisation options
- Subscription
- Affiliate/referral to brokers/conveyancers/insurance
- Sponsored lead-gen (careful: trust-sensitive)
