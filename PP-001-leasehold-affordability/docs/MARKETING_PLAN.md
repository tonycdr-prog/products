# LeaseholdCalc — First-week Marketing Plan

## Positioning
"The leasehold affordability calculator that includes service charge." Clear, single-purpose, trust-first.

## Distribution channels (5)
1. Reddit: r/HousingUK, r/UKPersonalFinance (post as a tool, not salesy)
2. TikTok/Instagram Reels: short demos comparing two flats w/ different service charges
3. SEO: long-tail pages (later) — “service charge affordability calculator”, “how much service charge is too much”
4. Partnerships: mortgage broker newsletters + conveyancer blogs
5. Communities: Facebook groups for first-time buyers / London flats

## First week tactics
- Day 1: ship landing + calculator; post on r/HousingUK with transparent MVP disclaimer.
- Day 2: create 3 comparison examples (e.g., £250 vs £600 service charge) as shareable screenshots.
- Day 3: outreach 20 brokers (UK) with “free client screening link” angle.
- Day 4–7: write 2 SEO posts (guides) + internal links to calculator.

## KPI targets
- 300 unique visitors
- 20 waitlist signups
- 5 broker conversations
