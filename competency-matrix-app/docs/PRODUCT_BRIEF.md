# Competency Matrix (Web)

**What it does:** Interactive competency matrix with filters, level selection, sign‑off fields, and CSV export. Saves locally in the browser.

**Target customer:** UK compliance and maintenance teams (smoke control, fire dampers, ventilation).

**Pricing model:** Free internal tool or bundled with IFC Automator.

**Competitive advantage:** Fast to fill, audit‑ready exports, and no spreadsheet chaos.
