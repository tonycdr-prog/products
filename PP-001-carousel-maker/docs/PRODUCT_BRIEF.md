# CarouselKit — Product Brief

## What it does
Creates LinkedIn-ready carousel slides with correct 4:5 sizing, simple themes, and one-click PNG exports. Eliminates the "resize Canva/Gamma" workflow.

## Who it’s for
- Solo founders and SaaS marketers
- Agencies creating social content for clients
- Content creators who post carousels weekly

## Core features
- Title + bullet input → auto-generated slides
- Themes (clean / bold / dark)
- Export as PNG
- Email capture / waitlist

## Pricing strategy
- Starter £9/mo (10 carousels)
- Growth £29/mo (unlimited)
- Team £79/mo (brand kit + multi-user)

## MVP constraints
No account system or storage yet. Exports are local downloads only.
