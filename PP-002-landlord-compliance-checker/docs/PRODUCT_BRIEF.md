# LetReady Checker

## What it does
Check whether the paperwork stack is ready before a tenancy dispute or Section 21 surprise.

## Who it is for
UK SMEs, freelancers, landlords, tenants or leaseholders with a first-line admin/compliance problem.

## Pricing strategy
Free checker for acquisition. £5/month Pro for saved cases, exports and reminders. £19/month Team for shared workspaces and audit trails.

## MVP scope
Single-page HTML/CSS/JS app with working calculator/generator, pricing and waitlist capture.
