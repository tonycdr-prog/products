# Marketing Plan — RepairTrail UK

## Landing page headline
Turn landlord chaos into a dated repair case file.

## 3 bullet benefits
- Build a factual repair timeline in minutes.
- Get a clear follow-up deadline based on issue severity.
- Copy or download a calm escalation letter with your evidence attached.

## CTA copy
Start free repair trail

## One distribution channel
Helpful Reddit/community replies in UK renter discussions, especially repair, damp/mould, heating, and letting-agent delay threads. Answer the question first, then offer RepairTrail as a free evidence-pack template.

## First validation push
1. Publish the static MVP as a simple hosted page.
2. Create three SEO/template pages: damp and mould repair letter, no heating follow-up, landlord not replying to repair request.
3. Share the free tool in 5 relevant UK renter discussions without overclaiming legal advice.
4. Track: visits, pack generations, copy/download clicks, waitlist email saves.

## Positioning guardrails
- Say “organise evidence” and “generate a factual letter”.
- Do not claim legal advice or guaranteed outcomes.
- Lead with privacy: prototype stores data locally in the browser.
