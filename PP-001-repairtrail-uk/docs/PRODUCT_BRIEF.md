# Product Brief — RepairTrail UK

## What it does
RepairTrail UK helps UK renters turn a repair dispute into a dated evidence pack: case details, severity, proof checklist, timeline events, suggested response deadline, and a copy-ready escalation letter.

The current static MVP works fully in-browser with local storage, demo data, copy-to-clipboard, and `.txt` export. No backend required for validation.

## Target customer
- Primary: UK private renters whose landlord or letting agent is slow to fix repairs.
- Secondary: tenant unions, housing charities, student advice services, and caseworkers who help renters organise evidence.

## Pricing model
- Free: one local repair pack with manual copy/download.
- Starter: £3/month for saved trails, reminder emails, PDF export, and multiple active repair cases.
- Adviser: £29/month for multi-tenant case management, branded exports, shared templates, and team workflow.

## Competitive advantage
- Evidence-first rather than generic letter-template-first.
- UK renter language and repair escalation workflow without making legal advice claims.
- Fast, private, zero-upload prototype: useful before trust is established.
- Natural expansion path from free template to reminders, PDF packs, and adviser dashboards.

## Chargeable v1 scope
- Account-backed saved cases.
- Email reminders for deadlines and follow-ups.
- PDF export with clean evidence timeline.
- Template variants for damp/mould, heating/hot water, security, leaks, pests.
- Stripe checkout and basic onboarding.
