# BookmarkNest — Product Brief

## What it does
BookmarkNest auto-categorizes bookmarks and generates a concise summary, making saved links searchable and easy to revisit.

## Target users
- Researchers, founders, and marketers saving lots of links
- Teams collecting references for projects
- Anyone with bookmark overload

## Core features (MVP)
- Paste list of URLs for instant categorization
- Summary of top categories + domains
- CSV export

## Pricing strategy
- Free: 200 links
- Pro: £8/mo unlimited + custom categories
- Teams: £29/mo shared collections

## Value prop
“Turn a chaotic bookmark list into clean, searchable collections in seconds.”
