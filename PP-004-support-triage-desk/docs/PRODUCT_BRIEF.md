# PRODUCT_BRIEF — TriageDesk

## What it does
TriageDesk is an inbox triage tool for B2B SaaS support teams. Users paste incoming tickets and instantly get:
- category + priority (P0–P3)
- estimated revenue risk
- SLA target recommendations
- owner suggestions
- response draft generation
- CSV export + standup summary copy

This iteration makes the MVP operational for real daily queue management in-browser (offline, no build step).

## Target customer
- SaaS founders and support leads handling 30–500 inbound tickets/week
- Teams with mixed billing/product/support requests and unclear escalation paths
- Customer success teams that need consistent triage without full Zendesk/Jira automation

## Pricing model
- **Starter (£19/mo):** Manual paste triage + exports
- **Growth (£59/mo):** Saved workspaces, custom rules, SLA dashboards
- **Scale (£149/mo):** Integrations (Zendesk/Intercom/Help Scout), team roles, audit trail

## Competitive advantage
- Fastest path from raw inbox text to ranked action queue
- Revenue-risk-aware triage (not just keyword tagging)
- Open-file MVP that works immediately without setup friction
- Practical operator outputs: owner assignment, SLA targets, response drafts, standup summary
