# Product brief — LeaseClauseCheck (PP-004)

## What it does
A deterministic “first pass” lease clause checker:
- paste lease text
- highlights common responsibility keywords (roof/lift/structure/windows/common parts/service charge)
- outputs a practical checklist + snippets

## Who it’s for
- Leaseholders trying to understand responsibility for expensive repairs
- RTM directors triaging questions from residents

## Pricing
- Free: keyword checks + checklist
- £25: exportable lease report + “questions to ask” pack

## Limitations
- Not legal advice
- Heuristic only; leases vary heavily
