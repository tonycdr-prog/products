# SMB Competitor Tracker

## What it does
A lightweight, browser‑based tracker for SMB operators to log competitor pricing, feature releases, and hiring signals. It stores everything locally (LocalStorage) and provides alerts when changes exceed thresholds.

## Target customer
- SMB SaaS founders and product marketers
- Boutique agencies monitoring 5–30 competitors
- Operators who need a simple, consistent cadence without setting up a heavy platform

## Pricing model
- **Free**: manual tracking for up to 10 competitors, export CSV
- **Pro (£19/mo)**: unlimited competitors, change alerts via email/Slack, weekly digest
- **Team (£49/mo)**: shared workspace, roles, and scheduled checks

## Competitive advantage
- **Zero setup**: runs in the browser, no integrations required
- **Focused signals**: pricing, features, hiring — the signals SMBs actually act on
- **Operator‑friendly**: manual snapshots encourage qualitative context, not just noisy scrapes
