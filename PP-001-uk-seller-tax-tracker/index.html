Does this work offline?
Yes. It’s a single page that runs in your browser and stores data locally on your device.

It’s not affiliated with HMRC. It’s a simple tracker to help you stay organised. If you’re unsure about your situation, speak to an accountant.
Can I use this for multiple tax years?
Yes. Your entries are labelled by UK tax year (6 April → 5 April). You can filter the view and exports by tax year.
