# Product Brief — Invoice Preflight Extractor

## What it does
Turns messy OCR/pasted invoice text into structured fields (vendor, invoice number, date, subtotal, VAT, total). Designed for SMEs who need accuracy before posting invoices to accounting systems.

## Who it’s for
- Small retail and e-commerce operators
- Bookkeepers handling multiple suppliers
- UK SMEs dealing with VAT fields

## Pricing strategy
- Starter £19/mo (200 invoices)
- Growth £49/mo (1,000 invoices)
- Pro £99/mo (unlimited + audit trail)

## Why now
Invoice volume is rising for small sellers, but capture accuracy is still painful. A lightweight preflight tool solves the immediate bottleneck without a full accounting suite.
