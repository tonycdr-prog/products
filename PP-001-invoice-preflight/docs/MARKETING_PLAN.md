# Marketing Plan — Invoice Preflight Extractor

## Channels
1. Reddit (r/smallbusiness, r/bookkeeping): share before/after accuracy results.
2. LinkedIn: short demo video showing field extraction.
3. Accounting blogs/communities: guest posts on invoice accuracy.
4. Google Search Ads: “invoice OCR accuracy” / “invoice capture tool”.
5. Partnerships: UK bookkeeping firms with volume clients.

## First-week tactics
- Post a 60s Loom demo with sample invoice.
- Offer free beta to 10 bookkeepers for feedback.
- Publish a checklist: “5 invoice OCR mistakes costing SMEs time.”
- DM commenters from the original thread with beta invite.
- Collect 3 testimonials and add to landing page.
