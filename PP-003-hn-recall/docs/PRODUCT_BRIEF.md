# HN Recall — Product Brief

## What it does
HN Recall is a memory‑friendly search layer for Hacker News. Users search with fuzzy prompts, apply filters (time, points, comments, category), save searches, and rerun them later.

## Target customer
- Power HN readers who routinely lose old posts
- Journalists, analysts, and researchers who need to relocate stories fast

## Pricing model
- Free: basic search + filters
- Pro (£3/mo): saved searches + alerts
- Teams (£12/mo): shared search library + exports

## Competitive advantage
- Optimized for “vague memory” queries with prompt templates and filters
- One‑click saved searches + history to reduce re‑querying
- Faster re‑discovery than vanilla Algolia search
