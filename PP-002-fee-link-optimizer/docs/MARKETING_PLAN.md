# Marketing Plan — FeeLink

## Channels
1. Reddit: r/smallbusiness, r/freelance, r/UKPersonalFinance
2. Facebook/LinkedIn groups for UK trades
3. YouTube shorts: "How much Stripe actually costs"
4. Partner with UK accountants/bookkeepers
5. Google search ads for "invoice payment link"

## First-week tactics
- Share fee calculator in UK SME groups
- Offer 1:1 fee audits to first 20 signups
- Create a 1-page comparison PDF
- Ship a demo payment link for a real invoice
- Collect 5 testimonials for landing page
