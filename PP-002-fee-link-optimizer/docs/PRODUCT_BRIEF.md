# Product Brief — FeeLink

## What it does
Calculates true payment fees and generates a payment link + QR so clients can pay with the lowest overhead.

## Target users
- UK freelancers and SMEs sending invoices
- Trades and services businesses with large average invoices

## Core value
- Clear net received per provider
- Faster getting paid via link/QR
- Reduces fee shock for high AOV invoices

## Pricing strategy
- Starter £19/mo
- Growth £59/mo
- Pro £99/mo

## MVP scope
- Fee calculator with editable rates
- Payment link + QR generator
- Waitlist capture
