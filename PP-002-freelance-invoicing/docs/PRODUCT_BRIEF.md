# Product Brief — Switchboard

## What it does
Lightweight freelancer CRM + invoicing replacement for Fiverr Workspace (AND.CO). Lets users import client data, create invoices, and manage billing in one hub.

## Who it’s for
Freelancers and small studios who relied on Fiverr Workspace and want a simple, affordable replacement without full accounting-suite complexity.

## Pricing model
Subscription SaaS: Starter £10/mo, Pro £18/mo, Studio £28/mo (20% annual discount).

## Competitive advantage
Purpose-built for the Fiverr Workspace shutdown with migration tooling, fast invoice creation, and a clean client hub that loads instantly without heavy accounting overhead.
