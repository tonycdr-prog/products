# ChaserFlow — Product Brief

## What it does
Automates polite invoice reminder sequences (UK‑friendly) so freelancers and SMEs get paid faster without manual chasing. Includes a lightweight tracker to calculate days overdue and generate ready‑to‑send emails.

## Target users
- UK freelancers, agencies, and small businesses
- Finance admins handling 10–200 invoices/month
- Bookkeepers who need a simple chaser tool without heavy accounting software

## Core workflow
1. Add invoices manually or import CSV
2. Set due dates + reminder cadence
3. Generate polite reminders with late‑fee/interest details
4. Track status (paid, unpaid, disputed) and next reminder date

## Pricing strategy
- Starter £10/mo (up to 20 invoices)
- Growth £25/mo (unlimited reminders + automations)
- Studio £60/mo (multi‑company)

## Competitive edge
UK‑first legal templates, polite tone controls, and ultra‑simple setup vs heavy accounting suites. Works offline (in‑browser) for quick wins.
