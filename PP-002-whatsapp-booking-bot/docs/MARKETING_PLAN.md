# MARKETING_PLAN — WhatsApp Booking Bot

## Channels
1. Reddit (r/smallbusiness, r/smallbusinessowner)
2. WhatsApp Business forums/groups
3. Google Maps outreach (salons/clinics)
4. Facebook groups for trades/services
5. Partner with booking agencies

## First-week launch tactics
- Post demo video + ask for beta testers on Reddit
- 30 cold emails to UK salons and tour operators
- Offer “done‑for‑you” setup for first 10 users
- Publish comparison vs. email/call booking
- Collect testimonials in week one
