# Product Brief — arXiv Quality Ranker

**What it does:**
Pulls citation data from OpenAlex for arXiv IDs and ranks papers by a simple quality score (citations + recency).

**Target users:**
Researchers, PhD students, and ML engineers scanning arXiv daily.

**Core features (MVP):**
- Paste arXiv IDs
- Fetch OpenAlex metadata
- Rank by quality score
- Waitlist capture

**Pricing strategy:**
- £9/mo individual
- £29/mo team (alerts + saved lists)

**Why now:**
ArXiv volume is exploding; relevance-only search isn’t enough.
