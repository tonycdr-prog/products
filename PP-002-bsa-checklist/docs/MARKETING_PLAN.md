# BSA Checklist — Marketing Plan

## First‑week launch tactics
1. Share a “BSA landlord certificate checklist” PDF on LinkedIn with CTA.
2. Post in UK facilities/property management groups.
3. Email small managing agents offering a free pilot.
4. Partner with conveyancing solicitors for referrals.
5. Publish a blog post: “Why BSA paperwork stalls property sales.”

## Distribution channels
- LinkedIn (UK property/facilities)
- Property management forums
- Email outreach to managing agents
- Partner referrals (solicitors)
- SEO blog content
