# BSA Checklist — Product Brief

## What it does
A UK‑specific Building Safety Act checklist and document tracker for landlords, managing agents, and leaseholders. It guides users through required steps and highlights missing documents.

## Target users
- Managing agents (small portfolios)
- Resident management companies
- Leaseholders managing sales compliance

## Core value
- Reduces stalled transactions due to missing certificates
- Centralises timelines and documents
- Clear audit trail for compliance

## MVP scope
- Checklist with progress tracker
- Simple document tracker
- Basic pricing + waitlist capture

## Pricing strategy
- £25/building/month
- Volume discounts for 5+ buildings

## Expansion ideas
- Automated reminder emails
- Document upload and sharing
- Built‑in BSA timeline templates
