# Product Brief — Cashflow Clarity

## What it does
A simple monthly cashflow + P&L tracker built for owners who want clarity without accounting fluff.

## Target users
- UK freelancers and solo operators
- Service businesses with simple revenue/expense streams

## Core value
- Fast monthly view of net cash
- Exportable for accountants
- Clear enough to replace spreadsheets

## Pricing strategy
- Starter £15/mo
- Growth £49/mo
- Pro £89/mo

## MVP scope
- Add income/expense by month
- Monthly summary + net chart
- CSV export + waitlist
