# Product Brief — SaaS Stack Map

## What it does
Lightweight inventory of all services behind your SaaS: domain registrars, hosting, email, databases, analytics, payments, and renewals.

## Target users
- SaaS founders running multiple products
- Agencies managing client stacks
- UK-first SaaS teams needing cost visibility

## Core MVP features
- Add services with owner, renewal, and cost
- Category filtering
- CSV export
- Waitlist capture + pricing

## Pricing strategy
- Starter £15/mo
- Pro £35/mo
- Agency £79/mo

## Next build steps
- Renewal email reminders
- Per-product workspaces
- SSO and team permissions
