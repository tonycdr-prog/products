# ServiceChargeSense — Marketing Plan (first week)

## Positioning
"Understand your service charge increase in 5 minutes. Red flags + a clean letter template."

## Landing page headline + CTA
- Headline: **Spot red flags in your service charge in 5 minutes.**
- CTA: **Run the audit**
- Secondary CTA: **Get updates**

## 3 key benefits
- **Sense-check the jump:** YoY % change + inflation-adjusted comparison
- **Know what to ask for:** red flags + prompts (breakdown, management fee basis, major works)
- **Act immediately:** copy/paste letter + PDF export for your records

## Distribution channels (pick one to start)
**Reddit: r/HousingUK**
- Lead with value (checklist + screenshots), link to the free checker
- Offer to iterate based on comments (“paste anonymised headline figures and I’ll improve the prompts”)

## First-week launch tactics
- Day 1: Post a “service charge red flags checklist” on r/HousingUK + link to free checker
- Day 2: Publish 3 SEO articles + embed the tool
- Day 3: Reach out to 20 UK leasehold-focused solicitors/accountants with a one-paragraph pitch
- Day 4: Create a downloadable PDF checklist (lead magnet)
- Day 5: Ask for 10 user interviews from commenters affected by spikes

## What to measure
- Completion rate of audit form
- % who download report PDF
- Waitlist conversion rate
- **Waitlist delivery rate** (endpoint success vs local-only saves)
