Section 20 notices: stop guessing. Get organised, fast.

This is a practical toolkit for UK leaseholders facing major works (lift replacement, roof, cladding, communal repairs). It helps you gather the right documents and generate a clear letter to the managing agent.

Important: This tool is not legal advice. It’s a structured way to request information and keep your timeline straight.

What you do next (checklist)

1) Identify what stage you’re at Notice of intention → estimates → award of contract. Ask for copies if you don’t have them.
2) Request the evidence pack Spec of works, tender list, estimates, survey reports, and the breakdown of your apportionment.
3) Check your lease wording What counts as “common parts”, and are lifts/roof/structure included for your unit?
4) Log dates + deadlines Consultation windows matter. Keep a single timeline and send everything in writing.
