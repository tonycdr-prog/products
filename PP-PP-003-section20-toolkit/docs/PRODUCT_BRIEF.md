# Product brief — MajorWorksMate (PP-003)

## What it does
A UK leaseholder “major works” toolkit:
- checklist for Section 20 / consultation stages
- document request checklist (“evidence pack”)
- letter generator to request specifics + apportionment basis

## Who it’s for
- Leaseholders facing large bills (lift replacement, roof, cladding, communal works)
- RTM directors who need a consistent communication workflow

## Pricing strategy
- Free: checklist + letter generator
- £25 Guided Pack: exportable letter pack + timeline + clause prompts
- Upsell later: £59/mo “Director mode” (annual planning + comms templates)

## Guardrails
- Clear disclaimers: not legal advice
- Encourage professional advice for high-stakes scenarios
