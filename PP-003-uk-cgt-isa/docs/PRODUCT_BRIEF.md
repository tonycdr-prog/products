# Product Brief — GIA Shield

## What it does
UK CGT calculator and Bed & ISA planner for GIA investors. Generates a clean summary for self‑assessment.

## Who it’s for
UK retail investors and advisors managing taxable accounts who need quick CGT estimates.

## Core features (MVP)
- CGT calculator (basic/higher rate)
- Bed & ISA sell amount suggestion
- Simple report output (screen/PDF later)

## Pricing strategy
- Free single calc
- Pro £6/mo unlimited scenarios
- Advisor £15/mo multi‑client exports

## Differentiation
- UK‑specific guidance and wording
- Designed for year‑end planning
