# TypeBack — Marketing Plan (Week 1)

## Channels
1. Hacker News (Show HN) — “Typed-text recall tool for when you didn’t copy”
2. Reddit (r/productivity, r/macapps) — pain posts about lost typing
3. Twitter/X — short demo clips
4. IndieHackers — build-in-public with privacy emphasis
5. DEV.to article — “Why clipboard history isn’t enough”

## First‑week tactics
- Publish 60‑second demo video
- Offer 30-day Pro trial to early adopters
- Collect feedback from accessibility communities
- Partner with keyboard shortcut blogs
- Share privacy architecture diagram
