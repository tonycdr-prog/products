# BoundaryCheck UK — Product Brief

## What it does
Estimates boundary discrepancies from title plan vs on‑site measurements, then generates a copy‑ready surveyor brief and evidence checklist.

## Refined target customer
- UK homeowners preparing for a sale or extension
- Small landlords managing boundary disputes or neighbour questions
- Property managers who need a quick compliance record

## Core features (MVP)
- Area discrepancy calculator (title plan vs fence)
- Surveyor brief generator
- Evidence checklist for photos + documents
- Email capture for Pro monitoring waitlist

## Pricing model (refined)
- **Free:** single calculation + surveyor brief
- **Pro:** £9/mo for saved reports, PDF export, and annual reminders

## Distribution tactic (realistic)
Publish a “UK boundary dispute evidence checklist” post and share in r/UKProperty, LandlordZONE forums, and NRLA member groups, linking to the free calculator as the first step.

## MVP constraints
No GIS upload or official Land Registry integration yet.
