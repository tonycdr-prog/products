# BoundaryCheck UK — Product Brief

## What it does
Estimates boundary discrepancies from title plan vs on‑site measurements and produces a surveyor brief + next‑steps checklist.

## Who it’s for
- UK homeowners
- Small landlords
- Property managers dealing with boundary confusion

## Core features
- Area discrepancy calculator
- Surveyor brief generator
- Checklist for evidence pack
- Email capture

## Pricing strategy
- Free single calculation
- £19 for report + checklist
- £49 for template letters + evidence pack

## MVP constraints
No GIS upload or official Land Registry integration yet.
