# Product Brief — Invoice Chaser Lite

**What it does:**
Automates polite invoice follow‑ups. Generates reminder emails and keeps a simple log so owners stop losing cash to awkward chasing.

**Who it’s for:**
UK freelancers, agencies, and small businesses who send 5–100 invoices/month.

**Core value:**
Get paid faster without uncomfortable manual chasing.

**Pricing strategy:**
£20/mo starter (50 invoices), £49/mo unlimited + templates. Optional add‑on: SMS reminders.
