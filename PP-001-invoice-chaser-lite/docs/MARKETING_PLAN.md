# Marketing Plan — Invoice Chaser Lite

**Channels (first 5):**
1. Reddit r/smallbusiness + r/freelance (value post: “templates + reminder cadence”)
2. Facebook groups for UK freelancers/agency owners
3. QuickBooks/FreshBooks app directories (if later integration)
4. LinkedIn posts: “stop chasing invoices manually”
5. Cold email to small agencies from Clutch/GoodFirms

**First‑week launch tactics:**
- Post a free reminder email template pack
- Share a short case study ("reduced overdue invoices by X days")
- Run a 10‑user beta with waived fees for testimonials
