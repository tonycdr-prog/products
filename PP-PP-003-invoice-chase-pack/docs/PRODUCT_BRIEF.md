# PayChase Pack — Product Brief

## What it does
Turns an unpaid invoice into a ready-to-send chase bundle:
- Calculates days late + rough statutory interest estimate
- Adds B2B fixed compensation bands (optional)
- Generates a Letter Before Action draft
- Outputs a PDF evidence checklist

## Who it’s for
- UK freelancers, agencies, contractors, micro-SaaS vendors
- Anyone repeatedly writing the same “please pay” escalation emails

## MVP scope
- Calculator (days late + interest estimate)
- Letter generator
- PDF export (client-side)
- Evidence checklist PDF

## Pricing strategy
- Free (calculator + copy/paste)
- £15 “pack” (PDF outputs)
- £9/mo Pro (reminder timeline + history + multiple debtors)

## Risks
- Needs careful legal wording + pre-action protocol alignment before public launch.
- Must clearly distinguish consumer vs B2B.
