# PayChase Pack — Marketing Plan (first week)

## Positioning
"Generate an LBA + PDF evidence checklist in minutes. Stop wasting time chasing payment."

## Distribution channels
1. Reddit: r/freelance, r/smallbusiness, r/Entrepreneur (value-first posts: checklist + template)
2. IndieHackers: “How I chase unpaid invoices” post + free generator
3. SEO: “letter before action invoice”, “late payment act interest calculator”, “small claims invoice”
4. Partnerships: accountants/bookkeepers serving UK SMEs
5. LinkedIn: short posts targeting agencies/consultants (cashflow pain)

## First-week tactics
- Ship a free “Late payment checklist” blog post with downloadable PDF
- Post an anonymised template + calculator link
- Ask 10 freelancers for feedback; add their missing fields

## What to measure
- Conversion to PDF download
- Copy-to-clipboard usage
- Waitlist signups
