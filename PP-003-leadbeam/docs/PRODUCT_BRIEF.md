# LeadBeam — Product Brief

## What it does
LeadBeam is a lightweight CRM that imports curated leads, tracks follow‑ups with next‑action dates, and generates discovery playbooks to find similar prospects. It replaces messy Notion + Gmail setups with a focused pipeline.

## Target customer (refined)
- UK‑based solo founders and micro‑agencies who manage 50–1,000 warm leads
- B2B service businesses (marketing, dev, ops) that live in spreadsheets today
- Small sales teams (1–5 people) that want clarity without a full CRM rollout

## Core features (MVP)
- Manual lead entry + CSV import
- Pipeline stages with status + next‑action reminders
- Lead notes and quick edits in‑table
- Discovery playbooks by industry/role/region
- Exportable CSV for sharing

## Pricing model (refined)
- **Free:** up to 50 leads, pipeline view, basic discovery checklist
- **Pro (£9/mo):** unlimited leads, CSV export, weekly follow‑up reminders, playbook templates

## Distribution tactic (realistic)
Publish a “Follow‑Up Rhythm for Small UK Sales Teams” checklist and share it in r/UKSmallBusiness, IndieHackers, and UK founder Slack groups, linking to the free demo as the first step.

## Competitive advantage
- 15‑minute setup with no admin overhead
- Built‑in discovery guidance (most CRMs only store data)
- UK‑first positioning with simple pricing and local workflows
