# LeadBeam — Product Brief

## What it does
LeadBeam is a lightweight CRM that imports curated leads, tracks follow-ups with next-action dates, and generates discovery playbooks to find similar prospects. It replaces messy Notion + Gmail setups with a focused pipeline.

## Target customer
- UK SMEs and solo founders
- Agencies managing prospect lists
- Sales teams under 5 people

## Core features (MVP)
- CSV import + manual lead entry
- Pipeline stages with status + next-action reminders
- Lead notes and quick edits in-table
- Discovery playbooks by industry/role/region
- Exportable CSV for sharing

## Pricing model
- Starter: £12/mo
- Growth: £20/mo
- Team: £45/mo

## Competitive advantage
- 15-minute setup with no admin overhead
- Built-in discovery guidance (most CRMs only store data)
- UK-first positioning with simple pricing and local workflows
