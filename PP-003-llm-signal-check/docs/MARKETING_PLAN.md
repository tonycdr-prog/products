# LLM Signal Check — First-week Marketing Plan

## Landing page positioning
**Headline:** “Find companies likely using ChatGPT / OpenAI — from public signals.”

**3 benefits (bullets):**
- Fast triage: score 10–15 domains in minutes
- Evidence-first: shows the exact string that triggered the signal
- Works even when crawling is blocked: Paste mode fallback

**CTA copy:**
- Primary: “Run a free check”
- Secondary: “Get the LLM Signals checklist”

## Distribution channels (pick 1 to start)
**Reddit (r/sales / r/SaaS):** post a short “how I find AI-adoption accounts” walkthrough + link to the tool + explicit caveats.

## First week tactics
- Day 1: ship MVP + waitlist.
- Day 2: publish “LLM usage signals checklist” as a PDF lead magnet.
- Day 3: post a case study: 20 domains scanned → 5 high-confidence hits → outreach template.
- Day 4–7: outreach 30 AI consultants with affiliate rev share for Pro.

## KPI targets
- 400 visits
- 25 waitlist signups
- 3 Pro demos
