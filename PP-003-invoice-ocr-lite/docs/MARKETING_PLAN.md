# Marketing Plan — Invoice OCR Lite

**Channels:**
1. Reddit r/smallbusiness (invoice processing thread)
2. UK accountant communities + LinkedIn groups
3. Shopify/WooCommerce seller communities
4. YouTube demo videos (before/after time saved)
5. Cold outreach to local retail chains

**First‑week tactics:**
- Offer free scan of 20 invoices for 10 beta users
- Publish an accuracy benchmark vs. common tools
- Collect real invoice samples to improve parsing
