# Product Brief — Invoice OCR Lite

**What it does:**
In‑browser OCR for invoices. Extracts vendor, date, and total, then exports CSV.

**Who it’s for:**
Small retailers and service businesses processing 20–500 invoices/month.

**Core value:**
Reduce manual data entry time and errors.

**Pricing strategy:**
£25/mo starter (200 invoices), £59/mo unlimited + bulk.
