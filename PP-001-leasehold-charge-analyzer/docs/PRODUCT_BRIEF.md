# PRODUCT_BRIEF

## Product
Leasehold Charge Stress Tester

## What it does
A browser-based UK leasehold affordability tool that models service-charge increases, forecasts cashflow pressure, and generates a challenge-prep note leaseholders can send to managing agents/freeholders.

## Target customer
- Primary: UK leaseholders hit by large annual/balancing/major-works service charge demands
- Secondary: Leasehold advisors, conveyancers, and resident association volunteers supporting disputes

## Pricing model
- **Free:** Single scenario, on-page risk output
- **Starter (£9/mo):** Saved scenarios + exportable stress-test report
- **Pro (£29/mo):** Portfolio mode, advisor sharing, branded PDF exports, priority support

## Competitive advantage
- Built for a **specific UK pain point** (service-charge shock), not generic budgeting
- Produces **actionable dispute prep output** (checklist + draft challenge note), not just a score
- Works instantly as a static/offline page; no account required for first value moment
