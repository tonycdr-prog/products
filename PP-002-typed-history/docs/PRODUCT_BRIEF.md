# PRODUCT_BRIEF — TypeBack (PP-002)

## What it does
Local-first typed text history that captures snippets as you type (not just clipboard) so you can recall lost text.

## Target users
- Developers and support teams
- Writers and researchers
- Anyone who often retypes lost snippets

## Core features (MVP)
- Capture typed snippets on Enter
- Search recent snippets
- Local-only storage (privacy-safe)
- Waitlist capture

## Pricing strategy
- Personal £4/mo
- Pro £9/mo (encrypted sync)
- Team £29/mo

## Differentiation
- Keyboard-level recall without clipboard dependency
- Privacy-first design
