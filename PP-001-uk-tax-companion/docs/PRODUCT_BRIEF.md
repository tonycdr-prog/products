# PRODUCT_BRIEF — TaxSnap UK

## What it does
TaxSnap UK is a lightweight self-assessment planning app for UK sole traders. It lets users:
- Track income and expenses in a simple ledger
- Estimate Income Tax, Class 2/Class 4 NI, and optional student loan repayment
- See a Payment on Account forecast and due-date schedule
- Export income/expense CSVs for accountant handoff

Runs as a single static page with local browser storage (no setup required).

## Target customer
Primary ICP: UK sole traders/freelancers (£20k–£120k annual turnover) who are not ready for heavyweight accounting software but still need confidence in tax reserves and filing prep.

Secondary ICP: early-stage accountants/bookkeepers offering low-touch advisory to solo clients.

## Pricing model
- Starter — **£15/mo**: estimator, ledger tracking, quarterly reserve guidance
- Pro — **£29/mo**: accountant export pack, reminders, filing checklist templates
- Accountant Pack — **£99/mo**: multi-client dashboard + branded exports

## Competitive advantage
1. **Fastest time-to-value:** starts in seconds; no onboarding wall.
2. **UK-specific clarity:** focuses on self-assessment + NI + POA for sole traders.
3. **Operationally tiny:** static-first architecture makes distribution/deployment trivial.
4. **Bridge product:** sits between spreadsheet chaos and full accounting suites.
