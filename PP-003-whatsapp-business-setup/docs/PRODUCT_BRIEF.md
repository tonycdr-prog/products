# PRODUCT_BRIEF — WhatsApp Business Setup Wizard

## What it does
Guides SMEs through a setup checklist and generates ready‑to‑paste greeting/away messages and quick replies.

## Who it’s for
Small businesses using WhatsApp Business but not configured (no auto‑replies, hours, catalogues).

## Core features
- Setup checklist + maturity score
- Auto‑reply generator
- Optional done‑for‑you setup service

## Pricing strategy
£15/mo for templates + audits, £79 one‑time setup.

## MVP scope
Front-end checklist + copy generator.
