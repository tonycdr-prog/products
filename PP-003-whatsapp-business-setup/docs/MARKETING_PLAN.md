# MARKETING_PLAN — WhatsApp Business Setup Wizard

1. Reddit (r/smallbusiness)
2. Facebook/WhatsApp Business groups
3. Local business chambers/newsletters
4. UK small‑business directories

- Reddit post: “Most SMEs don’t configure WhatsApp Business — here’s a free checklist.”

- Short Loom demo of the setup wizard
- Offer 10 done‑for‑you setups at £49 to seed testimonials
