# AICodeHire — Product Brief

## What it does
Generates AI-aware hiring scorecards with role-specific rubrics, tasks, and policy guidance.

## Who it’s for
- Engineering managers and founders hiring developers
- UK startups updating hiring practices for AI-assisted coding

## Core features
- Role + stack-specific scorecard generator
- AI policy templates (allowed/partial/restricted)
- Task packs (build, debug, review)

## Pricing strategy
- Team £49/mo
- Studio £149/mo

## Differentiation
- Explicit AI workflow evaluation
- Lightweight and fast to use
