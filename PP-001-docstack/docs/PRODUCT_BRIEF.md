# DocStack — Product Brief

## What it does
DocStack combines proposals, contracts, and invoices into a single workflow for small businesses.

## Target customer
Freelancers and small agencies (1–20 people) who currently juggle 2–3 tools for client documents.

## Core features
- Proposal builder with templates
- One‑click contract conversion
- Invoice generation from accepted proposal
- PDF export + email send (MVP placeholder)

## Pricing strategy
- Starter £29/mo
- Pro £59/mo
- Team £99/mo

## Differentiation
Unified workflow + zero re‑entry of client data.
