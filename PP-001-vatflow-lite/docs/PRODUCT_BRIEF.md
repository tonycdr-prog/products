# PRODUCT_BRIEF — VATFlow Lite

## What it does
VATFlow Lite is an offline-first UK VAT invoice simplifier for freelancers and small businesses. Users can build invoice line items, auto-calculate net/VAT/gross totals, run invoice sanity checks, and estimate statutory late-payment interest + fixed compensation.

## Target customer
- UK freelancers, consultants, and micro-agencies issuing 3–40 invoices/month
- Small businesses that need quick VAT confidence without opening accounting software
- Bookkeepers who want a lightweight pre-flight check before sending invoices

## Pricing model
- **Free:** Manual invoice builder + VAT/late-fee calculator + local save/load
- **Pro (£12/month):** PDF export, reusable templates, branded invoice notes
- **Team (£39/month):** Shared workspaces, audit history, client reminders

## Competitive advantage
1. **Faster than full accounting suites** for “send this invoice now” workflows
2. **UK-specific late payment estimate** (interest + compensation) in the same view
3. **No setup/no build/no login** MVP that works instantly in-browser
