# PlanPulse — Product Brief

## What it does
PlanPulse tracks AI subscription plans and shows daily comparisons across vendors.

## Target customer
Builders and teams choosing between AI subscriptions or needing accurate plan details.

## Core features
- Plan comparison table
- Daily change log
- Alerts for plan updates
- CSV export (MVP placeholder)

## Pricing strategy
- Free read‑only tier
- Pro £12/mo
- Teams £39/mo

## Differentiation
Always‑updated data instead of static blog posts.
