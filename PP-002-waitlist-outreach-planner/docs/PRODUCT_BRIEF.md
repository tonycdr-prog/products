# Product Brief — Organic Waitlist Planner

**What it does:**
Generates a compliant, organic outreach plan for founders who can’t post promo content in communities. Includes channel suggestions, messaging angles, and a task checklist.

**Target users:**
Early-stage founders, indie hackers, and solo operators launching MVPs.

**Core features (MVP):**
- Input product + audience, auto-generate outreach plan
- Export tasks CSV
- Waitlist capture

**Pricing strategy:**
- £29/mo for plan generator + templates
- £79/mo for community database + tracking

**Why now:**
Paid ads are expensive; community rules have tightened. Founders need a compliant plan to launch.
