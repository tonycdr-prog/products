# TelemetryGuard — Product Brief

## What it does
TelemetryGuard generates PR-time telemetry checklists and OpenTelemetry starter snippets so teams stop shipping uninstrumented code.

## Target users
- Small dev teams without dedicated observability engineers
- SaaS teams that repeatedly miss logging until incidents

## Core features (MVP)
- Checklist generator from endpoints/components
- Coverage score
- Language-specific OpenTelemetry starter snippets

## Pricing strategy
- Free: 1 service
- Team: £39/mo
- Enterprise: £149/mo

## Value prop
“Know what telemetry you’re missing before you merge.”
