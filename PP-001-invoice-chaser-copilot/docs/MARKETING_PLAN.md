# Marketing plan

## Landing page headline
Chase late invoices without sounding desperate.

## Three bullet benefits
- Calculate UK commercial late-payment interest and compensation in seconds.
- Generate friendly, firm, and final-notice emails from one invoice form.
- Save a local case pack with dates, amounts, next steps, and copy-ready wording.

## CTA copy
Build my free chase plan

## Primary distribution channel
SEO-led calculator pages for long-tail UK searches such as “statutory interest late invoice calculator”, “polite overdue invoice email UK”, and “late payment compensation small business”. Pair with short LinkedIn demos for freelancers/bookkeepers showing the before/after email transformation.

## First paid conversion test
Add a Stripe payment link for “Pro export pack” once 10 users submit/waitlist from the free calculator. Paid feature: branded PDF export + reminder calendar file for £5/month or £9 one-off.
