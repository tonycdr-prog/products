# Invoice Chaser Copilot

## What it does
Invoice Chaser Copilot helps UK freelancers and small businesses turn an overdue invoice into a practical chase plan. It calculates days overdue, estimates statutory interest using base rate + 8%, adds the fixed late-payment compensation band where the user marks the debt as B2B/commercial, and generates three copy-ready emails plus a case pack.

## Target customer
- UK freelancers chasing late client invoices.
- Micro-business owners without a credit-control process.
- Bookkeepers/accountants who want a lightweight first-line tool for clients.

## Pricing model
- Free: one-off browser-based chase plan and emails.
- Pro £5/month: cloud saved cases, branded PDF exports, calendar reminders, CSV invoice import.
- Team £19/month: shared workspace, audit trail, template management, accountant/bookkeeper handoff.

## Competitive advantage
Most invoice tools create invoices or generic reminders. This focuses on the emotional and procedural pain after non-payment: exact wording, escalation timing, statutory-charge context, and a record pack. It is small enough to be trusted, fast enough for one overdue invoice, and UK-specific enough to rank against generic SaaS invoicing pages.

## Current MVP scope
Static self-contained HTML app with working calculator, date logic, staged email drafts, local saved cases, text export, pricing section, and legal/financial-advice disclaimers. No server, no external dependencies.
