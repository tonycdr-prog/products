# Safe Lead Scout — Product Brief

## What it does
Builds compliant lead discovery workflows without auto‑DM spam. Generates search links across Reddit/HN/IndieHackers, qualifying checklists, and polite outreach templates.

## Who it’s for
- Freelance devs and agencies
- Sales teams avoiding social bans
- Founders doing manual outreach

## Core features
- Keyword + community search link builder
- Qualification checklist
- Outreach template builder
- Email capture

## Pricing strategy
- Starter £12/mo (50 searches)
- Pro £39/mo (unlimited + template library)
- Team £99/mo (shared inbox)

## MVP constraints
No scraping or automated messaging. Manual review only to stay compliant.
