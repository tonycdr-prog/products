# MARKETING_PLAN — WhatsApp Booking Assistant

## Channels
1. Reddit outreach (r/smallbusinessowner, r/smallbusiness)
2. WhatsApp Business Facebook groups
3. UK tourism operator forums + Facebook groups
4. Direct outreach to tour operators (Google Maps + TripAdvisor)
5. Partnerships with small CRM/booking agencies

## First-week launch tactics
- Day 1: Post demo + problem/solution on r/smallbusinessowner with personal story angle
- Day 2: 20 direct emails to UK tour operators with 1‑page demo link
- Day 3: WhatsApp Business group post + ask for beta testers
- Day 4: Short Loom demo + LinkedIn post tagged “tour operators”
- Day 5: Collect 5 pilot users, offer 30‑day free trial
