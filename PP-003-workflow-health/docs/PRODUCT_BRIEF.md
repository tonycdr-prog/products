# Product Brief — Workflow Health Checker

## What it does
Scores automation workflows for breakage risk and generates a self-healing checklist. Targets SMEs whose DIY automations fail on edge cases.

## Who it’s for
- E-commerce operators using Zapier/Make
- Non-technical owners with brittle automations
- Ops teams without full IT support

## Pricing strategy
- Starter £29/mo (health score + checklist)
- Growth £79/mo (alerts)
- Managed £299/mo (we maintain workflows)

## Why now
API changes and bad input data are breaking workflows daily. A lightweight health layer is easier to adopt than full replatforming.
