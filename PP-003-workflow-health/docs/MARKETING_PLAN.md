# Marketing Plan — Workflow Health Checker

## Channels
1. Reddit (r/smallbusiness, r/automation): “stop broken zaps”.
2. LinkedIn: post about automation failure costs.
3. Zapier/Make community forums.
4. YouTube tutorial: “5 fixes for broken automations”.
5. Partnerships with small automation agencies.

## First-week tactics
- Offer free workflow audit to 10 users.
- Publish a downloadable “self-healing checklist”.
- DM commenters from the original thread.
- Run a 30-minute webinar for owners.
- Collect 3 case studies with time savings.
