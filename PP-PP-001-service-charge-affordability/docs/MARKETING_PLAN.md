# Marketing plan — ServiceChargeSense (PP-001)

## Positioning
“Mortgage calculators ignore service charges. This one doesn’t.”

Secondary hook: “Stress-test a leasehold flat before you offer.”

## Channels (5)
1. Reddit: r/HousingUK, r/UKPersonalFinance — value-first posts (“Here’s a free calculator + what to ask the agent”).
2. SEO: long-tail queries like “service charge affordability calculator”, “leasehold service charge how much is too much”.
3. Partnerships: mortgage brokers / buyer’s agents (embed widget / referral).
4. TikTok/YouTube shorts: quick examples of a flat with £450/mo service charge changing affordability.
5. Lead magnets: “Leasehold Service Charge Red Flags Checklist” (PDF) gated by email.

## First-week launch tactics
- Day 1: Post to r/HousingUK with a breakdown + link + ask for feature requests.
- Day 2: Post to r/UKPersonalFinance with “copy/export buyer report” demo (already shipped).
- Day 3: Create 3 SEO pages (calculator + guide + FAQ).
- Day 4: DM 20 UK brokers offering a free co-branded PDF export.
- Day 5: Add “Section 20 risk” checkbox + update post.
