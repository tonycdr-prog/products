# Product brief — ServiceChargeSense (PP-001)

## What it does
A UK-focused affordability calculator for leasehold buyers that **includes service charges and ground rent** (plus a simple stamp duty estimate) so buyers don’t underestimate monthly costs.

## Who it’s for
- First-time buyers looking at leasehold flats
- Buyers comparing multiple flats with different service charges
- Mortgage brokers who want a quick breakdown that includes leasehold costs

## Core workflows
1. Enter property price, deposit, rate, term
2. Enter service charge + ground rent
3. See total monthly cost + SDLT estimate
4. Share link / export report (text report shipped in MVP)
5. Stress-test scenario (+rate shock / +service charge increase) to see risk

## Pricing strategy
- Free: calculator + share link
- One-off: “Buyer Report” (£25) export + red-flag checklist
- Pro: £59/mo for RTM directors/leasehold owners (templates + benchmarking + planning packs)

## Notes
- SDLT implementation is simplified in the MVP; production should use a maintained UK ruleset and region toggles.
- Current MVP is single-file + offline-friendly (no external CDN deps).
