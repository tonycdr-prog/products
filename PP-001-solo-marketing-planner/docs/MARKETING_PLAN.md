# SoloSignal — Marketing Plan (First Week)

## Landing page
**Headline:** “Plan your marketing week in under 5 minutes.”

**Three benefits:**
- Turn one goal into a 7‑day execution plan with tasks.
- Track KPIs without a CRM or agency.
- Export your plan for accountability and coaching.

**CTA copy:** “Generate my plan” / “Start planning now”

## One primary distribution channel
**IndieHackers (build‑in‑public + template drop)**
- Share a screenshot of the task board + KPI tracker
- Offer a free downloadable plan JSON for first 25 replies

## First‑week launch tactics
- Post a “solo founder 7‑day marketing plan” template as a free resource
- Record a 30‑second demo GIF showing plan generation + export
- DM 10 solo founders for feedback + testimonials
- Pitch a guest post to a founder newsletter with the template
