# Product Brief — COD Commission Tracker

**What it does:**
Calculates commissions owed on cash‑on‑delivery orders, flags unpaid commission, and creates a monthly summary for marketplace operators.

**Target users:**
Marketplace founders, multi‑brand platforms, COD-heavy e‑commerce operators in emerging markets.

**Core features (MVP):**
- Paste CSV orders, compute commission due
- Outstanding commission summary
- Per‑order breakdown
- Waitlist capture

**Pricing strategy:**
- Starter £19/mo (up to 1,000 orders)
- Growth £49/mo (up to 10,000 orders)
- Enterprise: custom

**Why now:**
COD remains dominant in many regions; commission reconciliation is manual and error‑prone.
