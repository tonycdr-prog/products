# FieldKit — Product Brief

## What it does
FieldKit is a lightweight CRM and job scheduler for local service businesses (plumbers, cleaners, electricians).

## Target customer
UK local service companies with 1–10 staff who want simple scheduling and billing without enterprise CRM pricing.

## Core features
- Client list + job cards
- Scheduling calendar
- Simple invoice generation
- SMS/email reminders (MVP placeholder)

## Pricing strategy
- Solo £39/mo
- Team £79/mo
- Pro £129/mo

## Differentiation
Focused on local services; minimal UI and quick setup.
