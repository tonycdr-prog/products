# Marketing Plan — PricePulse

## Channels
1. Reddit: r/smallbusiness, r/ecommerce, r/FulfillmentByAmazon
2. IndieHackers: build-in-public + weekly update
3. Shopify community forums
4. YouTube short demos: "10 hours saved per week"
5. Cold outreach to UK ecommerce agencies

## First-week tactics
- Post a one-minute demo video + free beta offer
- Offer 20 manual onboardings to seed testimonials
- Collect competitor tracking CSVs and return "pricing action" report
- Set up a simple landing page with waitlist and pricing
- Share 3 case studies (before/after time saved)
