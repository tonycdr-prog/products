# Product Brief — PricePulse

## What it does
Quickly turns a competitor price list into clear pricing actions and alert-ready exports. Helps small ecommerce sellers avoid hours of manual checking.

## Target users
- Small ecommerce operators (20–200 SKUs)
- Amazon/Walmart/Shopify sellers

## Core value
- Saves 10+ hours/week
- Immediate pricing guidance
- Easy CSV export to automate alerts later

## Pricing strategy
- Starter £29/mo (50 products, weekly checks)
- Growth £79/mo (200 products, daily checks)
- Pro £149/mo (500 products, hourly checks)

## MVP scope
- Web-based price action calculator
- CSV export for alerts
- Waitlist capture
