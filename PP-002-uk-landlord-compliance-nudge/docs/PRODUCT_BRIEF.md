# PRODUCT_BRIEF

## Product
**Compliance Nudge UK** — an offline-first landlord compliance workspace for tracking certificate deadlines and compliance evidence across multiple properties.

## What it does
- Tracks each property’s CP12, EPC, and EICR expiry dates
- Captures key compliance states (deposit protection + How to Rent evidence)
- Calculates per-property risk score and portfolio health summary
- Generates a ranked action queue for urgent compliance tasks
- Produces reusable reminder message templates for tenants and contractors
- Exports portfolio data (CSV/JSON)

## Target customer
- UK self-managing landlords (1–25 properties)
- Small letting agencies needing a lightweight compliance cockpit

## Pricing model
- **Starter (£9/mo):** up to 5 properties, core tracker + reminders
- **Portfolio (£29/mo):** up to 30 properties, exports + priority queue
- **Agency (£89/mo):** unlimited properties, team seats, audit logs (planned)

## Competitive advantage
- Purpose-built for UK landlord compliance workflow (not generic task management)
- Action-first UX: risk scoring + immediate next steps
- Works instantly in-browser with zero setup; no backend required for MVP demos
