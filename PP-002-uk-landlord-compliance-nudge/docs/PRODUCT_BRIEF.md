# PRODUCT_BRIEF — Compliance Nudge UK

## Product
Compliance Nudge UK is a lightweight compliance control panel for UK self-managing landlords who need a fast way to prevent certificate and tenancy-proof misses.

## Refined Target Customer
- **Primary:** UK self-managing landlords with **2–25 properties** who do not use a full property management suite.
- **Secondary:** Small letting agencies with **1–5 staff** still relying on spreadsheets, inbox reminders, and contractor notes.
- **Core pain:** CP12, EPC, EICR, deposit protection, and “How to Rent” proof live in different places, creating avoidable legal exposure and expensive last-minute bookings.

## Core Outcome
In under 5 minutes, a landlord can:
1. Enter one property.
2. Get an instant risk score across CP12, EPC, EICR, deposit protection, and “How to Rent” evidence.
3. Copy a ranked action checklist and reminder template.
4. Save the latest check locally for follow-up.

## Pricing Model
- **Free Tier (£0):** single-property risk check, ranked action checklist, and basic reminder template.
- **Pro Tier (£9/month):** up to 25 properties, weekly deadline reminder queue, export-ready compliance summary, and priority risk ordering.

## Why This Can Win
- UK-specific compliance workflow instead of generic task software.
- Action-first UX: risk score, evidence gap, and next step in one screen.
- Fast onboarding: no integrations or login required to get first value.
- Clear upgrade path: landlords feel the pain as soon as they manage more than one property.

## Real Distribution Tactic
Publish a free “1-property landlord compliance risk checker” post in UK landlord communities, especially **r/UKLandlords**, UK property Facebook groups, and landlord newsletter sponsorship swaps. Lead with the free checker, ask for feedback on missing certificate types, and collect waitlist signups for the £9/month Pro reminder queue.

## MVP Scope
- Static, self-contained `index.html` with vanilla HTML/CSS/JS.
- Working risk checker for CP12, EPC, EICR, and tenancy proof status.
- Copyable reminder template.
- Local browser save.
- Pricing section with Stripe placeholder and Pro waitlist capture.

## Guardrails
Compliance Nudge UK is a practical organiser, not legal advice. Copy must avoid claiming legal certainty and should prompt users to verify requirements with qualified advisers when needed.
