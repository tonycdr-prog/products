# PRODUCT_BRIEF

## Product
**Compliance Nudge UK** — a lightweight compliance control panel for UK self-managing landlords who need a fast way to prevent certificate and tenancy-proof misses.

## Refined Target Customer
- **Primary:** UK self-managing landlords with **2–25 properties** who do not use a full property management suite
- **Secondary:** Small letting agencies (1–5 staff) managing legacy spreadsheets and calendar reminders
- **Core pain:** deadlines are split across inboxes, notes and agents, which creates avoidable legal exposure and expensive last-minute booking fees

## Core Outcome
In under 5 minutes, a landlord can:
1. Enter one property
2. Get an instant risk score for CP12, EPC, EICR, deposit protection and “How to Rent” evidence
3. Receive a clear ranked action checklist and reminder template

## Pricing Model (MVP)
- **Free Tier (£0):** single-property risk check + action checklist + basic reminder template
- **Pro Tier (£9/month):** up to 25 properties, weekly deadline reminders, priority risk queue, export-ready summary

## Why This Can Win
- UK-specific compliance workflow instead of generic task software
- Action-first UX (risk score + next step) rather than passive date storage
- Fast onboarding: no integrations required to get first value

## Real Distribution Tactic (Initial)
**Channel:** UK landlord communities on Facebook + Reddit (especially r/UKLandlords)

**Execution:** launch with a free “1-property compliance risk checker” post, invite landlords to test their own address scenario, then capture waitlist signups for Pro via the £9/month positioning.
