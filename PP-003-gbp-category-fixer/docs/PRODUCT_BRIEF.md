# GBP Category Fixer Product Brief

## What It Does
Most local businesses describe themselves in Google one way while customers search another. Generate a practical category, services, and review prompt plan.

## Who It Is For
SME operators, freelancers, and agencies with an urgent recurring workflow that is currently handled in spreadsheets or ad hoc chats.

## Pricing Strategy
£19/mo single location, £79/mo agency 10-pack. Keep free audit ungated; charge for saved history, alerts, multi-client exports, and templates.
