# PRODUCT_BRIEF — AmberNote (PP-001)

## What it does
Lightweight notes app with OCR search for images and receipts, designed as an affordable Evernote alternative.

## Target users
- Freelancers and small business owners
- Consultants collecting receipts, cards, and notes
- Price-sensitive Evernote users

## Core features (MVP)
- Note creation + local archive
- OCR on images (Tesseract.js)
- Search across note text + OCR
- Waitlist capture

## Pricing strategy
- Personal £5/mo
- Pro £12/mo
- Team £39/mo

## Differentiation
- OCR-first for receipts/cards
- Simpler UI and lower price point
