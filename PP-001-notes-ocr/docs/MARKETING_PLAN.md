# MARKETING_PLAN — AmberNote (PP-001)

## 5 Distribution Channels
1. Reddit r/Evernote + r/selfhosted (value-focused posts)
2. YouTube comparisons: “Evernote alternatives”
3. AppSumo / lifetime deal communities
4. SEO content: OCR notes, receipt management, business card OCR
5. Freelance communities (Upwork, Indie Hackers)

## First-week launch tactics
- Day 1: Publish landing + demo, collect 20 emails
- Day 2: Post comparison chart vs Evernote pricing
- Day 3: Share OCR demo video on X + LinkedIn
- Day 4: Ask 10 freelancers for feedback
- Day 5: Pitch “Affordable OCR notes” to newsletters

## Positioning message
“All the OCR search you need, none of the Evernote price.”
